THE MISERY MARKET

A WORD FROM DR. ELIAS MORRAN

You were told to endure your misery. Wear it. Bury it. Pretend it makes you stronger. The Misery Market was built to prove a different truth—if pain is inevitable, it might as well be quantifiable.

Here, regret, panic, loneliness, and dread are no longer abstract ghosts. They are measurable assets, traded in units, indexed, charted, and leveraged. Some sell to feel lighter. Some buy to feel something at all.

I did not build this exchange to save anyone. I built it to expose the economy that was always there: the quiet transactions of guilt, fear, and longing you already make every day.
